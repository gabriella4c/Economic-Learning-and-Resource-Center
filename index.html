Welcome to NEW HOPE WORSHIP CENTER

We exist in order to Evangelize, Restore and Empower individuals globally with the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ. We are Gaining Souls for the Kingdom through Evangelism, Restoration and Empowerment. We are looking forward to seeing you in one of our services.

Welcome

Excellence & Honesty

In our world that is constantly changing, it is comforting to know that there is always a hand willing and able to reach out to help others. In times of crisis, moments of need, when people have nowhere else to turn; we are there with helping hands to bring balance to lives, warmth to families and a smile to children’s faces.

Ministries

We endeavor to meet the needs of the lost, hurt, needy and bring them into a vital and living relationship with God without regards to denomination, culture or ethnicity. We lead by spiritual example as we serve our community, our nation and our world with compassion, integrity and character in the spirit of excellence.

Ministries

Brother2Brother

Brother2Brother is committed to igniting, equipping, and uniting men through involvement in the church. Being a man is not easy, but the Bible does teach that there are spiritual resources available to help men face the challenge. God has given His Spirit, His Word, and one another so we don't have to do the job alone - we have our brothers!

B2B hosts a group activity on the third Wednesday night of each month, at the church, from 7:00 - 8:30 p.m.

Women of Purpose

Fulfilling our Destiny, walking by Purpose, and abiding by Biblical Principles to maximize our God given potential. We endeavor to see women of all ages and backgrounds come together to share their lives and encourage each other in spiritual growth, social involvement, friendship and sisterhood. The ministry stresses the importance of experiencing true power through a personal and intimate relationship with God and to demonstrate God’s love and care in our daily walk.

WOMEN OF PURPOSE MEETS: Every 3rd Wednesday @ 7:00 P.M

Sunday School

Our mission is to To carry out the Great Commission and harvest the family. We provide our young children, youth and adults with age- appropriate, inspirational biblical teaching in small groups for practical Christian living. The Sunday School department provide several activities and opportunities throughout the year to engage our members and to showcase their diverse talents. The children will receive genuine care, friendship, love, and sometimes food and gifts! Classes are grouped according to age starting from 3 years old.

Every Sunday @ 10:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.

Youth @ Promise

Empowering and educating the youth by assuming a holistic approach geared towards the development of the mind, body and soul, in preparation for service in the church and community. The NHED YOUTH Program offers recreational/ cultural/ academic clubs and activities, leadership development, recreational field trips, educational field trips, Team Facilitator mentorship, Team Facilitator supportive services, and academic support. We also offer small group and individual tutoring in the areas of Reading, Writing, Mathematics and Technology.

Our Team

Bishop Michael L. Thomas

Bishop Michael L. Thomas is the Senior Pastor of New Hope Worship Center of Miramar. He is a man after the heart of God with an intense desire to see others grow in the grace and the knowledge of the Lord Jesus Christ. He is a dynamic, energetic, evangelistic, and an anointed conference speaker who preaches the good news of salvation, restoration and empowerment in an undiluted and uncompromising way.

With a conspicuous anointing of God on his life, Bishop Thomas’ ministry has brought the message of salvation, deliverance and healing to multitudes. One of his greatest desires is to see souls be born to the kingdom. He ministers the Word of God with clarity, precision, and practicality to edify the Body of Christ and to equip the believers for the work of the ministry. He has traveled throughout the USA and abroad declaring the life-changing gospel of Jesus Christ.

Bishop Thomas was born in Clarendon, Jamaica. He accepted the Lord as his personal savior at the age of sixteen and is completely sold out to His cause. He was first ordained as a minister in the Church of God, then later earned a Master and Doctoral degrees in Theology from Trinity Theological Seminary.
